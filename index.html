Before I start

Content Comes From You

I build the site. Everything that goes on it including the writing, the photos, and the details come from the student. I don't write on anyone's behalf.

Intake form

Once we've agreed on what the site should include, I'll send over a form that asks for everything I need to get started like bio, activities, photos, links, and anything else going on the site. There's no rush on it. When it's filled out and sent back, I get to work. If anything is unclear or missing, I'll ask before moving forward.
The process

How It Works

A general idea of what to expect from start to finish.

1
We figure out what you need
Reach out and we'll talk through what the site should include, how many pages, and what it should look like. Once we're on the same page, I'll give you a clear price.
2
Half upfront
Half the agreed amount is due before I start. The other half is due when the site is done and ready.
3
You fill out the intake form
I'll send a form that walks through everything I need. text, images, links, and any other details. You fill it out at your own pace and send it back. That's the content the site is built from.
4
I build and show you a preview
I build the site first and show it to you before anything goes live. Once you're happy with it, we move to the next step.
5
The student sets up a GitHub account
When the site is ready to go live, the student will need a free GitHub account. Their username becomes their web address. I'll walk through the setup if needed. it takes a few minutes.
6
The site goes live on their account
I move everything over to the student's GitHub and publish it there. If you've gotten a custom domain, I'll connect it at this point. The second half of the payment is due here, and then I step away. The site is theirs.
